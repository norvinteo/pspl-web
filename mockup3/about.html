Our Story

35 years of marble mastery and unwavering commitment to excellence

From Humble Beginnings to Industry Leadership

What started as Jin's Brother Renovation in 1989 has evolved into Singapore's most trusted name in marble care. Our journey from a small family business to the preferred partner of luxury hotels and Grade A offices is a testament to our commitment to excellence.

Today, Polishing Specialist Private Limited stands as a beacon of quality in the marble restoration industry, combining traditional craftsmanship with cutting-edge technology to deliver results that exceed expectations.

With over 500,000 square meters of marble restored and maintained, we've earned the trust of Singapore's most prestigious properties through consistent delivery of exceptional results.
