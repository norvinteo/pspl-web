The PSPL Legacy

Two generations. One unwavering standard.

Three Decades of Mastery

For over three decades, PSPL has evolved from Jin's Brother Renovation Contractor into Singapore's premium stone restoration specialist. Two generations. One standard: perfection.

Today, Aaron Ang leads with both traditional craftsmanship and modern innovation.

Our Journey

1989

The Beginning

Founded as Jin's Brother Renovation Contractor by our patriarch. Started with residential HDB projects, building reputation through word-of-mouth and exceptional craftsmanship.

1995

Specialization Begins

Shifted focus to marble and stone restoration. First major conservation project at a heritage shophouse sparked our passion for preserving Singapore's architectural legacy.

2000

Commercial Expansion

Entered commercial sector with first corporate office project. Established partnerships with major contractors and architects.

2005

Expertise Recognition

Became trusted consultants for law firms and property developers. Our expertise in stone defect analysis set new industry standards.

2010

Next Generation

Aaron Ang joins the family business, bringing fresh perspective while honoring traditional craftsmanship. Introduced digital documentation and modern project management.

2015

Landmark Projects

Completed restoration of An-Nahdhah Mosque and multiple Grade A office buildings. Established as Singapore's premium restoration specialists with fixed-price guarantees.

2020

Pandemic Resilience

Three senior craftsmen with 30+ years each retired gracefully. Despite challenges, maintained all projects and client relationships. Zero retrenchments, 100% commitment.

2024

New Chapter

Incorporated as Polishing Specialist Private Limited. 35 years of excellence, 10,000+ projects completed, ready for the next generation of stone mastery.
