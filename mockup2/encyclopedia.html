Professional Overview

Carrara White marble represents the pinnacle of Italian stone craftsmanship, quarried from the same mountains that supplied Michelangelo's sculptures. This premium marble features distinctive grey veining against a pure white background, making it the preferred choice for Singapore's most prestigious corporate spaces.

Corporate Applications

Ideal for hotel lobbies, executive office reception areas, and luxury retail spaces where elegance is paramount. The sophisticated appearance justifies its premium positioning and maintenance requirements.

Technical Characteristics

Low porosity (0.12%) provides good stain resistance when properly sealed. The metamorphic structure offers excellent polishing capability, achieving mirror-like finishes that reflect light beautifully in architectural spaces.
