// ESTABLISHED 1989

What started as Jin's Brother Renovation Company in 1989 has evolved into Singapore's most trusted name in premium marble restoration.

Founded by our patriarch with nothing but determination and a commitment to excellence, we've spent over three decades perfecting our craft. Today, as Polishing Specialist Private Limited, we're the go-to specialists for Singapore's most prestigious properties.

Our journey is simple: We charge double because we deliver triple the value. When others say "impossible," we say "give us 48 hours."
