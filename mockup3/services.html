Complete Marble Restoration

Bringing marble back to its original glory

Surface Restoration

Advanced diamond polishing techniques restore the mirror-like finish of your marble surfaces. We eliminate scratches, dullness, and wear patterns while preserving the stone's natural beauty.

  • ✓ Diamond grinding and honing
  • ✓ Crystallization for lasting shine
  • ✓ Lippage removal for even surfaces

Structural Repair

Expert repair of cracks, chips, and structural damage using color-matched epoxy resins and traditional stone masonry techniques. Invisible repairs that restore structural integrity.

  • ✓ Crack injection and stabilization
  • ✓ Chip repair with color matching
  • ✓ Joint regrouting and sealing

Revive faded or discolored marble with our color enhancement treatments. We restore the stone's natural vibrancy while protecting it from future discoloration.

  • ✓ Stain removal treatments
  • ✓ UV protection application
